Aaron's Landmark

Seward Park

Seward Park has always been a significant part of my life. Ever since elementary school, I have been creating memories here.

This website has been made in exploration of this park located in the Lower East Side, bordering Chinatown.

The actual park is just a normal looking park. There are jungle gyms and a bunch of other equipment kids can play with but there is also a volleyball net and basketball courts.

With all these fond memories, I have written an essay narrating a story, which is located in the Esasy tab above.

If you wish to check out where this park is located, click the maps section. If you want to learn more about the courts, click the courts section.
